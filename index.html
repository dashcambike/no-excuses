No Excuses

The bike lane is not for cars.

  • Why?

It's About Safety

Our safety is put at risk for your convenience. The bike lane is used by the most vulnerable among us. People pedaling bicycles, using power wheelchairs, and riding scooters all rely on the this lane to be clear.

Don't Ask Us To "Go Around"

It's not safe. Drivers don't expect bikes in the road when there's a bike lane. People die. Kids die.

It's Never "Just a Minute"

Maybe you're here for just a minute. Then the next driver is. Then the next. Be a good neighbor.

It's Illegal

First and foremost, don't do it because you put our lives in danger for your convenience. It's also illegal. If you received one of our stickers, it is likely that someone has already recorded your license plate with their bike dashcam. At their discretion, they may have reported it to your local parking authority.
